About this project

The Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor Riser is a sturdy and stylish addition that elevates your monitor to a more comfortable viewing height. Placing your monitor at eye level has the potential to improve your posture and make you more comfortable while at work, even helping to reduce the strain on your hand.

Featuring artisan craftsmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your monitor to allow you to store pens, and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
